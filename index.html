About NoteNest

Learn what NoteNest is, why it was built, and who it is for.

📖

What is NoteNest?

NoteNest is a mobile-first web platform designed to help students easily share and access academic study materials in one centralized place. The platform allows students to upload, browse, and download educational resources such as lecture notes, PDFs, assignments, and study guides.

🎯

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a centralized platform where students can support each other academically by sharing useful resources. We believe peer-to-peer learning is one of the most powerful ways to grow — and NoteNest makes that effortless, free, and accessible to every student, anywhere.

🛠️
